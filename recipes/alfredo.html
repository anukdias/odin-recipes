Fettucini Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 Cups Heavy Cream
  • 1 Heaping Cup Parmesan
  • 3 Tbsp Butter
  • 1 Bag Fettucini
  • 1 Head Broccoli
  • 15 Shrimp
  • Drizzle of Olive Oil
  • 1/4 Tsp Salt
  • Pepper to taste
  • Italian Seasoning to taste

Steps

  1. Wash broccoli and detail shrimp
  2. Boil pasta
  3. While pasta drains, add butter to pan on low
  4. Add garlic and frozen shrimp
  5. Add italian seasoning, salt, and pepper while shrimp cooks
  6. Once shrimp is mostly cooked, add broccoli
  7. Add heavy cream
  8. Once heavy cream is bubbling at edges mix in cheese
  9. Mix pasta back in and serve 😄
