Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Flour
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Tbsp Butter
  • 1/2 Tsp Sugar
  • 1/4 Tsp Salt

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in microwave
  2. Crack egg and mix in dry ingredients
  3. Mix in butter until everything is well mixed
  4. Add in milk and flour slowly, mix lightly
  5. Let batter sit for at least 5 minutes
  6. Heat pan over medium-low heat
  7. Cook the pancakes and enjoy! 😄
